Fruit Salsa with Cinnamon Chips

Ingredients:

  • Approximately 1 cup each of diced strawberries, kiwis, pineapples, and mangos
  • Squeze of lime juice to taste.
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves, chopped.
  • 4-6 tortillas for cinnmon chips.
  • Melted butter for brushing.
  • Cinnamon sugar for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Dice the strawberries, kiwis, pineapples, and mangoes and mix them together in a bowl.
  2. Add a squeeze of lime juice and chopped fresh mint leaves to the fruit mixture.
  3. Cut the tortillas into triangles, brush them with melted butter, and sprinkle cinnamon sugar on top.
  4. Bake the cinnamon chips in the oven until crispy.
  5. Serve the fruit salsa with the cinnamon chips.

Rainbow Veggie Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas or lettuce leaves, depending on the desired number of wraps
  • Approximately 1/2 cup of hummus or cream cheese
  • Colorful vegetables such as shredded carrots, bell peppers, cucumbers, avocados, and spinach leaves, as per personal prefenece

Instructions:

  1. Lay out tortillas or lettuce leaves on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a layer of hummus or cream cheese on each tortilla or lettuce leaf.
  3. Place shredded carrots, bell peppers, cucumbers, avocados, and spinach leaves on top of the spread.
  4. Roll up the tortillas or wrap the lettuce leaves, securing them with toothpicks if necessary.
  5. Slice the wraps into smaller portions, if desired.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

Caprese Skewers

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes, approximately 12-15
  • Mozzarella balls (bocconcini), approximately 12-15
  • Fresh basil leaves, approximately 12-15
  • Drizzle of balsamic glaze to taste
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Thread cherry tomatoes, mozzarela balls, and fresh basil leaves onto skewers in an alternating pattern
  2. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the skewers
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Arrange the skewers on a serving platter or plate
  5. Serve as an appetizer or side dish

Citrus Infused Water

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Oranges
  • Lemons
  • Limes
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Fill a pitcher with water
  2. Slice oranges, lemons, and limes into rounds or wedges
  3. Add the citrus slices to the pitcher of water
  4. Optionally, add ice cubes for extra chill
  5. Stir gently to release the flavors
  6. Let the water infuse in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving
  7. Pour into glases and enjoy refreshing citrus-infused water
